01 — Manifesto
The Reflection
Every act is a mirror. We sign the ones who aren't afraid to stare back.

We build sound that refuses to be background noise — image, obsession, and frequency, fused until the artist and the myth are the same thing.

NARCISSIST MUSIC GROUP was built for artists who treat their own reflection as a battlefield. We don't manufacture a moment and move on — we sit inside it, distort it, and hand it back louder than it arrived.

The Underground, made visible. Dark trap, distorted vocals, lyrical shadow-work — we sign the frequencies other labels are too polished to touch, then give them the muscle to travel: production, visual identity, release strategy, and distribution, all under one roof.

We don't chase trends. We build the artist until the trend has no choice but to follow.
